Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC)

Want to go to GHC for free?

Every year, the top 15 most active members of Women in Computing (WIC) at KU have the chance to go
to the Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC) for free!

  • What is GHC?
    • The Grace Hopper Celebration is the world’s largest gathering of women and non-binary technologists.
  • Why should I attend?
    • It offers unparalleled networking opportunities with peers and industry leaders. You can attend inspiring keynote speeches, panel discussions, and technical sessions and gain access to internships at top companies like Amazon, Microsoft, Apple, Uber, Los Alamos, CIA/FBI, and USAA.
  • How do I become a top 15 member?
    • This is determined by a point system, where you earn points for attending general meetings, website meetings, Girl Code events, the KU hackathon, and more. The Vice President tracks the points, and at the end of the year, the 15 members with the most points are chosen to go.
  • If I was selected to attend one year, can I be chosen again?
    • Yes, as long as you're one of the top 15 members each year, you can be chosen to attend multiple times!
  • What expenses are covered if I attend?
    • Everything! Your transportation, hotel stay, conference registration, and meals are all taken care of.
